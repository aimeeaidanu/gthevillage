FAQs

How do I sign up?

  1. Go to the create account page
  2. Choose your desired sign up method*
  3. Complete the questionnaire
  4. Check for a success notification
  5. Go back to the login page and login with your credentials

*Recommended method: Sign up with google

"You haven't signed up yet" notification

Make sure you have actually completed the questionnaire. If you didn't complete the questionnaire, you won't be allowed to create an account. If that's the case, simply redo the questionnaire again and your account should be created.

Is there a fee with the service?

There is no fee with the service. Everything is completely free.

How does the matching process work?

We take all the data that you submitted through the questionnaire and compare that with other user's data.

When will I get my matches?

We'll email you when we find a match. You'll be able to contact them on the "matches" page.

What if I don't like the matches I received?

You can press the pass button next to their name card on the "matches" page
